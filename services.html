Cardiology

Expert Heart Care at Every Stage

Our Cardiology Department offers a complete spectrum of heart care — from early detection and risk assessment to advanced interventional procedures. Our catheterisation laboratory is equipped for coronary angioplasty, stenting, and electrophysiology studies, while our non-invasive unit provides echocardiography, stress testing, and Holter monitoring. Every patient receives a personalised cardiac care plan guided by internationally trained specialists.

Neurology

Advanced Brain & Nervous System Care

MedCare's Neurology Department is home to a dedicated stroke unit, epilepsy monitoring unit, and neuromuscular clinic. Our neurologists bring subspecialty expertise in stroke medicine, epilepsy, movement disorders, and neuroimmunology — supported by state-of-the-art neuroimaging, EEG, and nerve conduction studies available on-site. We also offer multidisciplinary care for patients with dementia, Parkinson's disease, and multiple sclerosis.

Paediatrics

Specialised Care for Every Child

MedCare's Paediatric Department offers comprehensive care for children from birth through 18 years. Our 12-bed Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU) supports premature and critically ill newborns, while our paediatric wards are designed to be calming, child-friendly environments that ease anxiety for young patients and their families. We offer subspecialty clinics in paediatric neurology, cardiology, infectious disease, and developmental medicine.

Diagnostics

Precise Diagnostics, Faster Answers

Our Diagnostic Imaging and Pathology Department supports every specialty at MedCare with cutting-edge imaging technology and an ISO-certified pathology laboratory. We offer the full range of radiological investigations — including 3 Tesla MRI, 64-slice CT, digital X-ray, ultrasound, and nuclear medicine imaging — alongside a comprehensive lab covering haematology, biochemistry, microbiology, and histopathology. Reports are delivered digitally and are accessible to referring clinicians in real time.

3 Tesla MRI (brain, spine, body)
64-slice CT scan
Digital X-ray & fluoroscopy
Diagnostic & obstetric ultrasound
Mammography & bone densitometry
Full blood count & biochemistry
Microbiology & culture sensitivity
Histopathology & cytology
