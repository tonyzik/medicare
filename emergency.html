Immediate Action

What To Do in an Emergency

Follow these steps calmly. Quick, clear action saves lives.

1
Call Our Emergency Line First

Dial +234 800 123 4567 immediately. Our dispatcher will guide you through the situation and alert the on-duty emergency team before you arrive.

2
Stay Calm & Keep the Patient Still

Avoid moving the patient unnecessarily, especially after a fall or accident. Keep them warm and reassure them that help is on the way.

3
Head Directly to Our Emergency Department

Our ED is open 24 hours, 7 days a week at 12 Hospital Road, GRA, Effurun. Walk-ins are admitted immediately — no appointment needed.

4
Bring Any Available Medical Information

If possible, bring the patient's ID, list of current medications, known allergies, and any recent medical records. This helps our team act faster.

First Response Guide

Do's & Don'ts While Waiting for Help

These guidelines apply while you wait for our team or while transporting the patient to our facility.

Do These
Keep the person calm, still, and warm until help arrives.
Perform CPR if the person is unresponsive and not breathing normally.
Apply gentle pressure with a clean cloth to control visible bleeding.
Loosen tight clothing, especially around the neck and chest.
Collect any medication containers or relevant items to bring to the ED.
Avoid These
Do not give food, water, or medication unless directed by our dispatcher.
Do not move a person with a suspected neck or spinal injury.
Do not leave an unconscious person alone.
Do not attempt to remove embedded objects from wounds.
Do not delay calling us — time lost in emergencies can cost lives.
